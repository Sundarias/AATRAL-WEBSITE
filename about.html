About Us

About the Institution

                Thanthai Periyar Government Institute of Technology is not merely another engineering college producing hordes of engineering graduates year by year. It is more than a center of learning and home to a diverse community of innovators and entrepreneurs. Our college is the fifth Government engineering college established by the Government of Tamil Nadu in July 1990, started uniquely from Fourth year to First year. Our campus spread over a sprawling 23 acres of land and is comfortably located away from the din and bustle of the city. The college offers UG courses in regular and part time mode and PG courses in regular mode. It is recognized by AICTE and affiliated permanently to Anna University, Chennai.

                 Our College is a premier institute of its kind, offering four year B.E. degree courses in five engineering branches, namely Civil Engineering, Electronics & communication Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, four post graduate courses viz M.E.(Applied Electronics) and M.E.(Manufacturing Engineering) of two years duration and M.C.A. of three year duration and Part time B.E. courses in Electronics & Communication Engineering and Mechanical Engineering and M.E.(Structural Engineering).

                Each department is housed in different blocks, namely, administrative block, civil engineering block, mechanical engineering block, Electronics and communication engineering block, Electrical and Electronics engineering block, Computer Science block, MCA block, science block and library block. Every department is endowed with stateof-art laboratories and airconditioned seminar hall. The college library is equipped with a good deal of books and national and international journals.

About the Department

                The Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) at Thanthai Periyar Government Institute of Technology, Vellore, has been offering a Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering since 2018. With a steadfast commitment to excellence, the department provides students with a strong technical foundation in cutting-edge areas of Electrical and Electronics Engineering, fostering innovation and shaping the future of technology. Electrical and Electronics Engineering is a dynamic and interdisciplinary field that integrates electricity, electronics, electromagnetism, communication, and computer programming into real-world applications. From pioneering advancements in renewable energy, smart grids, and automation to enhancing industrial, healthcare, and consumer electronics, EEE plays a critical role in technological evolution. Our program is designed to equip students with in-depth theoretical knowledge and hands-on experience, enabling them to excel in diverse sectors such as:
        • Power generation, transmission, and distribution
        • Electrical design
        • Automation
        • Renewable energy and smart grid technologies
        • Electronics and communication industries
        • Public sector undertakings and government organizations

                With a total intake capacity of 60 students, the department ensures a focused and enriching learning environment, fostering both academic excellence and professional growth. The EEE Department is equipped with modern laboratories, advanced research facilities, and industry-relevant tools to provide students with hands-on training and exposure to contemporary technologies. Our state-of-the-art laboratories include:
        • Electrical Machines Laboratory
        • Power Electronics and Drives Laboratory
        • Electronic Devices & Circuits Laboratory
        • Microprocessors and Microcontrollers Laboratory
        • Power System Simulation Laboratory
        • Renewable Energy and Smart Grid Laboratory

                These facilities enable students to work on real-time projects, research initiatives, and industry collaborations, enhancing their problem-solving skills and technical expertise. The department is deeply committed to academic rigor, research innovation, and holistic student development. Through a curriculum that blends core engineering principles, hands-on training, and industry exposure, we ensure that our graduates are well-prepared for both higher education and professional careers. Key academic and professional development initiatives include:
        • Project-Based Learning: Encouraging students to engage in innovative projects that address real-world engineering challenges.
        • Seminars & Technical Workshops: Hosting expert-led sessions on emerging technologies, industry trends, and research advancements.
        • Industry Internships & In-Plant Training: Collaborating with leading industries to provide hands-on training and real-world exposure.
        • Industrial Visits: Enabling students to gain practical insights into industry operations and technological advancements.
        • Student Research & Publications: Motivating students to undertake research projects, publish papers, and participate in national and international conferences.
        • Soft Skills & Professional Ethics Training: Integrating value education, leadership skills, and professional ethics into the curriculum to shape well-rounded professionals.
